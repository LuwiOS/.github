# SUPPORT.md
## Getting Help with LuwiOS

We're here to help you get the most out of LuwiOS. Here are the best ways to get support:

### Community Support
- **GitHub Discussions**: Ask questions, share ideas, and connect with other users.
  - [LuwiOS Discussions](https://github.com/luwios/luwios/discussions)
- **Discord Server**: Join our real-time chat for instant help.
  - [LuwiOS Discord](https://discord.gg/luwios) (invite link)
- **Matrix Room**: For decentralized communication.
  - `#luwios:matrix.org`

### Documentation
- **Official Documentation**: Full guides, installation instructions, and troubleshooting.
  - [docs.luwios.org](https://docs.luwios.org)
- **Wiki**: Community-maintained tips and advanced usage.
  - [LuwiOS Wiki](https://github.com/luwios/luwios/wiki)

### Reporting Issues
If you've found a bug or regression:
- Search [existing issues](https://github.com/luwios/luwios/issues) first.
- Report new bugs using the [Bug Report Template](.github/ISSUE_TEMPLATE/bug_report.md).

### Feature Requests
Have an idea for LuwiOS?
- Submit it via the [Feature Request Template](.github/ISSUE_TEMPLATE/feature_request.md).

### Commercial Support (Coming Soon)
For enterprise or mission-critical deployments, contact us at **support@luwios.org**.
