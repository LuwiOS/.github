## Code of Conduct

LuwiOS is committed to fostering a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free community for everyone.

### Our Pledge
We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, and healthy community.

### Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Being respectful of differing opinions and experiences.
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism.
- Using inclusive language.
- Showing empathy toward other community members.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment or discrimination based on identity.
- Trolling, insults, or personal attacks.
- Unwelcome sexual attention.
- Publishing private information (e.g., email, phone).

### Enforcement
Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project team at **conduct@luwios.org**. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly.
